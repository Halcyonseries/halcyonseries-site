ABOUT HALCYON

In the farthest reaches of a distant future, Halcyon chronicles the daring expeditions of elite starship crews venturing beyond the known boundaries of space. Their missions lead them to uncharted worlds, encounters with enigmatic alien civilizations, and the unraveling of profound cosmic mysteries. Yet, beneath the surface of an era defined by unwavering optimism and interstellar unity, a darker, unsettling truth quietly whispers from the void—not all that awaits discovery will be welcoming.

Halcyon is an original 3D-animated sci-fi adventure series currently being developed as a solo project. All aspects of production—including conceptual design, modeling, animation, and rendering—are handled entirely in Blender. Every starship, alien species, and distant world is meticulously crafted, resulting in a universe driven by exploration, scientific authenticity, and captivating storytelling.
